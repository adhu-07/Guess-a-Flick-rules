Guess-a-Flick

The Ultimate Emoji Guessing Game

Game Flow

  • The game is played by **10 teams** in rotating turns.
  • When it is your team's turn, you have **10 seconds to choose** a category (Movie or Song) and a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard).
  • After a choice is made, an emoji clue appears and a **30-second timer** begins.
  • The turn passes to the next team after the timer expires or a guess is submitted.

Scoring and Difficulty

  • **Easy** clues are worth **1 point**.
  • **Medium** clues are worth **3 points**.
  • **Hard** clues are worth **5 points**.
  • Each team can only choose a difficulty level once per turn.

Spelling and Naming Rules

  • Answers are **not case-sensitive**, so "FROZEN" and "frozen" are both correct.
  • Spelling must be **correct**. For example, "Toy Story" is correct, but "Toy Storry" is not.
  • If a movie or song name starts with **"The"**, it must be included in the answer to be counted as correct.
